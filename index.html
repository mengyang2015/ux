Design Philosophy

Simplicity

I am striving for simplicity, and it isn't always easy, especially for enterprise softwares which involve complicated user flows. As simplicity is the ultimate sophistication, it is a journey that I would like to embrace for the rest of my career.

Customer Insights

I am always a strong customer/user advoate. I leverage customer insights from in-depth customer research and help to drive product roadmap and feature prioritization. I am also good at user experience metrics.

Data Visualization

I'm a strong believer in the power of data visualization, which can also serve as an effective design communication tool, and I try my best to leverage visual communication whenever I can.
